Pop Culture: Seth's Collection

Seth Kieley is a collector from Blackmans Bay, Tasmania, who has estimated a value of $10,000 from over 250 Pop! Vinyl figurines.

Mr. Kiely says his obsession with the Vinyl characters begun after he received his first model of Daryl Dixon, from the hit series The Walking Dead, as a gift from his partner in 2017.

"It's highly addicting. Every character is different, and some are worth hundreds of dollars. Once I started collecting, I couldn't stop, " he said.

After prices for the collectibles began to peak over a five-year phase, Seth has continued to sell, buy and trade the figurines in a large online community across the country, housing over 12,000 members.

Pop! Vinyls can cost anywhere from between $20 to well over $1,000. With Seth's addiction continuing to grow, the space available in his home is beginning to lessen.

The couple have been forced to move the valuable figures into a private room, which is quickly becoming overcrowded.

His encouraging partner commented on her boyfriend's hobby: "I'm okay with Seth's collection. It makes him happy. He knows how to handle his money and that's why I don't mind his dedication to the Vinyls. "

"I'll probably end up selling or trading them until they're gone," says Mr Kiely. "That won't be for quite some time; they'll most likely rise in price again in the near future. "

Seth says that the best way to start collecting the figures is to enter raffles and competitions as they are usually some of the cheapest ways to win big.
